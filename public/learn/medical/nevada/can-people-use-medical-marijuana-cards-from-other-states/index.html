Can people use medical marijuana cards from other states in Nevada?

Answered 2019-05-17T04:00:00+0000 by Wesley McQuillen

Yes. Nevada is the only state in the country that will recognize medical marijuana cards from other states.

Patients from California, Arizona and anywhere else medical marijuana is legal will be able to shop at Nevada dispensaries while visiting.

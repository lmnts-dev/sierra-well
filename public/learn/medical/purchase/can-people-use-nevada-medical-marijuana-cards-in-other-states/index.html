Can people use Nevada medical marijuana cards in other states?

Answered 2019-05-17T04:00:00+0000 by Wesley McQuillen

It depends on the state, and the regulations.

Currently, Arizona, Delaware, Maine, Michigan, New Hampshire and Rhode Island allow patients from other states to use marijuana, but generally not buy it.
